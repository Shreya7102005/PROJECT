About SafeHer

SafeHer is an AI-based platform designed to enhance women's safety during travel. It allows users to verify the safety of cabs and auto-rickshaws based on previous user reviews and complaint analysis.


Our mission is to create a safer environment where women can travel confidently without fear. SafeHer empowers users by providing real-time safety insights and emergency support features.

How to Use SafeHer

  1. Verify Ride: Enter the vehicle number in the "Verify Ride" section to check if the ride is safe based on previous reports.

  2. Add Review: After your ride, share your experience by adding a review. Mark it as Safe, Unsafe, or Harassment to help others.

  3. SOS Alert: In emergency situations, click the SOS button to alert your contacts instantly.

  4. Fake Call Feature: Use the fake call option to simulate a call when you feel unsafe.

  5. Stay Aware: Always check reviews before starting your journey to ensure safety.
